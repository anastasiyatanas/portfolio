About us!

My amazing husband and I are newly weds, I am the primary photographer and photo editor! My talented husbands, Artem is the primary videographer! We had a wedding in March that was postponed due to Covid. We decided to elope, as we loved eachother so much and wanted to be with eachother ever minute of every day. We eventually were able to have our wedding at one of our parents houses in October, it was such an amazing day! We understand how hard it is to find a photographer and videographer, and we want to help others to make the process a lot smoother and easier! We also understand how stressful planning a wedding can be, read why we love elopments here!


Anastasiya (Ana)
